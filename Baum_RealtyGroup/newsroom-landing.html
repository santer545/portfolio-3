Baum Arranges Sale of NNN Leased Walgreens Encumbered by a Self-Liquidating Loan in Northwest Missouri for $5.3MM

CHICAGO, IL (December 16, 2014) – Chicago-based Baum Realty Group, LLC (Baum), one of the Midwest’s leading retail brokerage firms, is pleased to announce the sale of a single tenant net leased Walgreens located in Maryville, Missouri, for $5,300,000. Walgreens has 20 years remaining left on the absolute NNN lease with 50 one-year options. Baum Vice Presidents Patrick Forkin and Brad Teitelbaum represented the seller in the transaction, a Missouri based private developer. The purchaser was a private investor on the West Coast who entered into the transaction as part of a 1031 exchange. Read more >
