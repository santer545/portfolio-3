TENANT REPRESENTATION

We set the national standard for client service.

Our brokers are dedicated to the long-term success of their clients. We work shoulder to shoulder with our clients to ensure the ideal location is found in the shortest possible timeframe under the most favorable terms and conditions.

Baum combines market knowledge, insight and hustle (ART) with the latest technology and information sources (SCIENCE).

This equal reliance on both ART and SCIENCE sets us apart from other firms. Our brokers are constantly developing and stewarding strong industry relationships affording them access to pre-market information on the best locations available. We are constantly investing in the latest technology to advance our clients’ goals. We provide each client with access to the most comprehensive set of tools in the industry, including state-of-the-art satellite imagery, custom aerial photography and demographic data.

We give our clients the tools to realize their market penetration & expansion plans.

We create evocative packages which illustrate relevant demographic data, market trends and new developments, combined against the backdrop of each client’s targeted market areas. Our tailor-made site packages contain overview and target market maps, fixed-wing and drone aerial photography, satellite imagery, market narratives, site logs complete with key site attributes, detailed site summaries and floor plans, digital panoramic photos and custom demographic analysis.

We train our brokers from the ground up to understand our ART and SCIENCE approach.

We train our brokers on how tenants thrive and how to make tenant real estate departments look good. Really good. This allows our team to locate ideal sites, expedite leasing transactions and ultimately maximize profitability for each client every time. The result? Many of our tenants have trusted us with their exclusive site selection needs for more than a decade.
